--------------------------------------------------

  strat and pretend you make this decision 100% of the time

  chosing to make this decision combined with your 
  opps strat 

3. REGRET = ACTUAL - EV - TOTAL
  (if you SHOULD do action X TOTAL should be bigger
  so you should chose total meaning your regret will be 
  positive)
